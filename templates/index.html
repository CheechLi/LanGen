LanGen

An educational platform

What is LanGen?

LanGen is an educational platform geared to children. 
With our product, you can write a prompt to 
generate a story & have the ability to 
listen to the story in your native 
language and a second, translated language!

Topics of Focus



01

Vocab and Grammar

The ability to understand vocabulary 
used within our story

02

Pronunciation

The ability for users to 
improve their pronunciation

03

Improved Literacy

The ability for users to 
improve their reading level by 
reading complex stories

04

Fun Stories

The ability to choose what kind 
of fun stories you can 
read based on text input

Our Mission

Our product aims to solve the issue of vanilla education by translating stories for children to both read & learn: vocabulary, grammar, & pronunciation of a chosen language. We hope to provide a fun, interactive learning experience for children of all ages.
